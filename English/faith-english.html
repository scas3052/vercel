Statement of Faith

Our core beliefs and values

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, serving as our ultimate guide for faith and practice.

All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness. - 2 Timothy 3:16

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ and His sacrificial death and resurrection.

For it is by grace you have been saved, through faithâ€”and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God. - Ephesians 2:8

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the presence and power of the Holy Spirit, who guides, comforts, and empowers believers.

But the Advocate, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name, will teach you all things. - John 14:26

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and service.

Now you are the body of Christ, and each one of you is a part of it. - 1 Corinthians 12:27

Service

We believe in serving others as an expression of God's love and our faith in action.

Serve one another humbly in love. - Galatians 5:13

Love

We believe in demonstrating God's love through compassion, acceptance, and care for all people.

Dear friends, let us love one another, for love comes from God. - 1 John 4:7

Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer as a vital connection with God, transforming lives and circumstances.

Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, present your requests to God. - Philippians 4:6

Mission

We believe in spreading the Gospel to all nations, making disciples and sharing God's love worldwide.

Go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. - Matthew 28:19

Sanctification

We believe in the ongoing process of sanctification, where believers are transformed into Christ's likeness through the work of the Holy Spirit.

May God himself, the God of peace, sanctify you through and through. - 1 Thessalonians 5:23

Repentance

We believe in true repentance as the foundation of our faith. It is a complete turning away from sin and turning to God with a transformed heart and renewed mind. Through genuine repentance, we experience God's forgiveness and the power to live a holy life.

If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just and will forgive us our sins and purify us from all unrighteousness. - 1 John 1:9
