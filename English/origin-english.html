Origin of Work

The journey of Holy Army Fellowship

1990

The Beginning

Holy Army Fellowship was founded with a vision to spread God's love and build a community of faithful servants dedicated to worship and service.

1995

First Church Established

Our first church was established in Mandavalli, marking the beginning of our physical presence in the community.

2000

Growth and Expansion

The fellowship expanded to multiple districts in Andhra Pradesh, reaching more communities with God's message.

2010

Community Outreach

Initiated various community service programs and spiritual retreats to strengthen our connection with the community.

Present

Continuing the Mission

Today, Holy Army Fellowship continues to grow and serve, with multiple churches across Andhra Pradesh, touching lives and spreading God's message of love and hope.
