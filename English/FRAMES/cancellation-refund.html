Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Holy Army Fellowship, we want to ensure your complete satisfaction with our products and services. This policy outlines our procedures for cancellations and refunds.

Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement
  • Cancellation requests must be made through your account or customer service
  • Orders that have been shipped cannot be cancelled
  • Custom frame orders cannot be cancelled once production begins

Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method

Return Conditions

  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • Custom frames are non-returnable unless damaged
  • Return shipping costs must be borne by the customer

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Report damages within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photos of the damage
  • Replacement or refund will be processed after verification
  • We cover return shipping for damaged items

Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom-made frames with personalized text
  • Items marked as non-returnable
  • Products without original packaging
  • Used or damaged items (unless received damaged)

Refund Process

  1. Initiate return request through your account
  2. Receive return authorization and shipping instructions
  3. Ship the item back in original packaging
  4. Refund processed after quality check
  5. Confirmation email sent once refund is processed
